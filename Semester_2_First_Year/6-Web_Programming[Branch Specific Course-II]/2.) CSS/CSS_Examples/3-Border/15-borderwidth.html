The border-width Property

This property specifies the width of the four borders:

5px: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

Medium: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

2px: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

Thin: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

15px: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

Thick: India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.

Note: The "border-width" property does not work if it is used alone. Always specify the "border-style" property to set the borders first.
